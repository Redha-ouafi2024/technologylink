Life on Our Planet:(episode 1)

The incredible story of life's epic, four-billion-year journey on Earth comes alive in this series from Steven Spielberg and the team behind "Our Planet."
Not as good as Prehistoric Planet but covers way more Although I was a little disappointed by the quality of the CGI compared to prehistoric planet, it is very fulfilling to have a longer period and many more types of species covered. I am very excited about that. I also like that they connect the continental context, and the atmosphere with the life that existed. I don't know enough to state the extent of how accurate or not the depictions are as the other reviewers mention, so I'll leave that up to the palaeontologists. However, it does a great job in exciting us viewers for the amazing history our planet has. Morgan Freeman's voice is very calming and relaxing.
